Why have goals?

Professionally, I'm working on my Product Design and Strategy craft. In the next 2 years I'd like to become a Product Design Manager. Before focusing on managing, I want to build more craft skills. I think that will make me a better manager. Personally, I'm working on a healthy balance and being outside more. Why have goals? If you don't know what your goals are you don't know what success looks like.
